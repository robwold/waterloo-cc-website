Waterloo Chess Club

Who are we?

Waterloo Chess club provides a welcoming place for all to meet and play chess. Although we're currently based in Crosby village, our members hail from across the North West. We've welcomed new members with Chess.com ratings below 1000, and our ranks have previously boasted an International Master, so whatever your previous experience you'll find someone to give you a game here. We play competitively under classical time conditions in competitions run by the Merseyside Chess Association, but people looking for a casual rapid game can usually find one.

Where and when?

We meet in The George pub at 7:15pm on Monday evenings. Members and guests are encouraged to use the bar in return for their hospitality. Visitors joining us for the first time are advised to get in touch using the link below before coming - just in case we're playing another team in a different venue that evening!

How do I get in touch?

Our club chairman Graeme Solomon will be very happy to answer any questions. You can contact him via this form on the English Chess Federation website.
