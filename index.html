Who are we?

Waterloo Chess Club provides a welcoming place for all to meet and play chess. Although we're currently based in Crosby village, our members hail from across the North West of England, and include complete beginners and titled players.

Where and when?

As of 08/06/26, we've moved to a new venue in Round Eight at 7:30pm on Monday evenings. Members and guests are encouraged to use the bar in return for their hospitality. Visitors joining us for the first time are advised to get in touch using the link below before coming — just in case we're playing another team in a different venue that evening!
